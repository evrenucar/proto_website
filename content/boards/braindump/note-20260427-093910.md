Finding small quantities of chromoly steel (often referred to as 4130 steel or *chroommolybdeen staal* in Dutch) tubing locally can be tricky, as general hardware stores do not carry it. You will generally need to go to specialized metal suppliers that cater to individuals and hobbyists, or order from specialist webshops.

**[Metaalcenter](http://googleusercontent.com/map_location_reference/0)**
Located in Den Hoorn, which is essentially right next to Delft. They are an online supplier with a physical location and they cater to both B2B and individuals.
* **Convenience:** Very close to Delft for easy pickup (Sionsdreef 5, Den Hoorn).
* **Selection:** They offer a wide variety of steel tubes, though you should call ahead to confirm if they have exact 4130 chromoly in stock or if they can source small pieces for you.

* **Services:** They can cut tubing to the exact lengths you need while you wait.

**Alternative: Specialized Online Suppliers**
Because chromoly 4130 is highly specialized (commonly used in custom bicycles, motorsport roll cages, and aerospace), many local metal shops only stock standard structural steel (like S235 or S355). If you specifically need 4130 chromoly tubes for a project, you might have the best luck ordering from specialist European suppliers who ship small quantities to the Netherlands, such as **25crmo4.com** (a Dutch specialist in chromoly tubing) or **Online Metals**.
